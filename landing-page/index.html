Spam sucks

You want to keep your email address secret so you don't get spam, but so many websites still want your email in order to register.

They promise they won't spam you, but nothing is keeping them from selling your address to people that will.

Many people end up creating multiple email addresses to combat this problem. But who can rememberall of those logins?

Use aliases to stop the spam
before it starts

OpenKasm allows you to instantly create email aliases that redirect to your normal email address.

Create an alias for each website registration, then just delete the alias if the website spams you or sells your address to spammers.

Banish spammers permanently
by deleting their alias

You will receive no more mail from the spammer or anyone he/she sold your address to.
